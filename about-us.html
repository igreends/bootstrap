About

Your Instructor  Dimitry Hunter

 

A highly skilled professional, Dimitry is a passionate and experienced tennis player and coach.

Dimitry comes to Chira Tennis Academy from Europe and has 10 years' experience as a tennis coach. He started playing tennis when he was 14 and has a diploma in Tourism and Sports from Kuban University, Russia.

He taught tennis at Armavir Sport Club in Europe for 3 years, Children and Junior Tennis School for 5 years, worked as a tennis coach in various summer camps. The past 2 years he has been coaching at CTA working with all levels of players from beginner to national. At CTA Dimitry manages all levels tennis players.
